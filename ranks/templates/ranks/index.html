Compilers Grouping

Here's what to do: For every person in the list, put a number between 1 and 10. Put 10 if you really want to be with that person, or 1 if you hate them. Your numbers don't need to sum to anything or be exclusive.

The program will look at all 35 of the possible groupings. Each grouping has a "group score." For every pair of people in a group together (A, B), if A submitted a value of x for B and B submitted a value of y for A, then A and B will together increase the group score by (x+y). The program will chose the grouping with the highest group score.
